‚úÖ Brainstorming & Ideation

‚úÖ Best Practice Example

"I'm writing an essay about climate change. Help me brainstorm 5 unique angles I could explore, focusing on lesser-known impacts." This uses AI as a thinking partner while you maintain control of the direction.

‚úÖ Research Assistance

AI can help you find relevant sources, summarize complex topics, and identify key concepts.

‚úÖ Best Practice Example

"Explain quantum computing in simple terms and suggest 3 academic sources I should read for deeper understanding." This helps you grasp complex topics while directing you to primary sources.

‚úÖ Study Aid & Tutoring

‚ùå Academic Dishonesty

‚ùå Bias Amplification

  • ‚ùå Using AI to take exams or complete assessments

  • ‚ùå Ignoring institutional AI policies
  • ‚ùå Using AI without disclosure when required

Finding the Balance

Ethical guidelines and best practices for responsible AI integration

üìö Lesson 4 of 5

üéØ The Golden Rules of AI-Assisted Learning

  • ‚úÖ Transparency: Always disclose when and how you've used AI
  • ‚úÖ Attribution: Cite AI tools just like any other source
  • ‚úÖ Verification: Always fact-check AI-generated information
  • ‚úÖ Learning Focus: Use AI to enhance, not replace, your learning
  • ‚úÖ Skill Development: Ensure you're still building core competencies
  • ‚úÖ Policy Compliance: Follow your institution's AI guidelines

üîÑ The AI Learning Workflow

1. Define Your Goal: What do you want to learn or accomplish?

2. Use AI as a Tool: Let AI assist, but maintain control

3. Verify & Validate: Check AI outputs against reliable sources

4. Reflect & Apply: Process the information and make it your own

5. Document Usage: Keep track of how AI assisted you
